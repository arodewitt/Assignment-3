The New
Green Book.

2017 Edition

When Victor H. Green and George L. Smith compiled the first “Green Book” now 80 years ago, they were creating a new survival tool. African Americans had long understood by 1937 that there were places and people they should avoid, times of dusk and night that weren’t accessible to them. But, as blacks took to the roads, that protective oasis of knowledge could not go along.

Designed and developed by Alicia DeWitt.
